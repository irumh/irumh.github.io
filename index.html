Research Paper ChemRxiv • 2026

Seeing Like a Structural Biologist: EyeCue, a Multimodal AI Agent for Interpreting MD Trajectories with Audio-Visual Explanations

We introduce EyeCue, a multimodal AI agent that analyzes MD trajectories to generate narrated video explanations highlighting key molecular behaviors, including binding, dissociation, and conformational transitions. EyeCue's interpretations show strong agreement with established quantitative metrics, establishing it as an expert-level interpreter of MD simulation outputs.
